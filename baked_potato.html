Baked Potato

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is perfect for parties! Everyone loves pizza, and your guests can customize their toppings to suit their personal tastes.

Ingredients

  • 4 russett potatoes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Sea salt
  • Choice of toppings

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven to 425Â°F
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  3. Use a fork to poke a few holes into the potatoes.
  4. Place on the baking sheet, rub with olive oil, and sprinkle liberally with sea salt all over
  5. Bake 45 to 60 minutes, or until the potato is fork-tender and the skin is crisp.
  6. Slice open each potato, fluff the insides, and serve with desired toppings.
